Discover Ivory Coast - Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Discover Ivory Coast

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the purpose of the website: to showcase the beauty, culture, and attractions of Ivory Coast to potential travelers. It is simple, memorable, and aligns with the goal of promoting tourism in the region.

Optional Domain: discoverivorycoast.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an engaging and informative travel guide showcasing Ivory Coast's rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and breathtaking natural landscapes. It will provide valuable travel insights, promote eco-tourism, and support local businesses in the tourism sector.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-visit tourist destinations in Ivory Coast?
  • What are the visa requirements and safety guidelines for traveling to Ivory Coast?

Color Schema

Primary (#2c3e50)
Secondary (#e74c3c)

Primary Color (#2c3e50): Used for headings, navigation bar, and footer.

Secondary Color (#e74c3c): Used for buttons, links, and accents.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, and general content.

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif

Usage: Headings and subheadings.
